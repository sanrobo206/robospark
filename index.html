About RoboSpark

RoboSpark is a comprehensive learning management system specifically designed to bridge the gap between theoretical robotics and industrial application. Founded by Sanatan Sinha, the platform provides a rigorous 8-part curriculum.

Through the integration of real-time syncing, behavior tracking, and an immersive assignment viewer, we ensure that every student has the tools to transform from a beginner to a robotics hero.
