About RoboSpark by Sanatan Sinha

RoboSpark by Sanatan Sinha provides a systematic path for robotics development. The curriculum begins with theory for microcontrollers, servo motors, and all other sensors. [cite: 129, 130]

After mastering those theoretical aspects, the focus shifts to learning CAD design on Onshape. This phase involves creating digital models of robot parts and assemblies. [cite: 133, 134]

The final stage is real robotics. This level involves integrating those 3D printed parts with the microcontrollers, servo motors, and sensors studied earlier. [cite: 140]
