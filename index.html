About RoboSpark by Sanatan Sinha

[cite: 127]
[cite: 128]

RoboSpark by Sanatan Sinha provides a systematic path for robotics development. [cite: 129] The curriculum begins with theory for microcontrollers, servo motors, and sensors[cite: 130]. Understanding these components ensures a solid foundation[cite: 131, 132].

After theory, the focus shifts to CAD design on Onshape[cite: 133]. This involves creating digital models to visualize structures before construction[cite: 134, 135, 136].

Following digital designs, the next step is 3D printing parts[cite: 137]. This transition from software to hardware is essential for prototyping[cite: 138, 139].

The final stage is real robotics, integrating 3D printed parts with microcontrollers and sensors for a functional robot[cite: 140, 141, 142, 143].
